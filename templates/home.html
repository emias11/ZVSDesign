I have always dreamt of creating an architectural studio. It would have a shopfront featuring alluring items of furniture, decorative, fine art and unusual objects. To me, architecture and decorating is the pursuit of what can make a space more interesting and harmonious.

Voilá, that's how ZVS Design was born.

We are a design practice focused on residential architecture and interior design with a unique flair for collectable furniture foremost of the 20th Century as well as curated vintage.

We take a fully immersive approach, designing and managing all aspects of architecture and interior design, down to the smallest detail. We advise on and source furniture, decorative objects and art that represent a variety of styles, eras and places of origin.

Regardless of the size of the project - whether designing an extension, reconfiguring the house interiors, creating a cool contemporary feel, kitchen or bathroom modern makeover - we work with our clients to create their unique home.

We have our 'shopfront' where collectable items are for purchase. Some of these are featured below and of course we can source others to suit our clients' requirements.

We like fusing the modern and classical - the contemporary and traditional - to create a sense of timeless elegance and calm.
